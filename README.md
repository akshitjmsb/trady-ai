# 🤖 Trady AI – Your Personal Wealth Bot

Trady AI is a secure, interactive Streamlit app that helps you analyze your investment portfolio using open-source LLMs via OpenRouter.

---

## 📦 Features

- Upload or manually enter your stock portfolio
- Choose from multiple LLMs (LLaMA 3, GPT-4 Turbo, Claude, etc.)
- Ask financial questions about your holdings
- Everything runs securely and privately on your local machine

---
